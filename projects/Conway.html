Conways Game of Life

The Game of Life is an example of a cellular automaton based on 3 rules:

  • A live cell dies if it has less than two neighbours.
  • A live cell dies if it has more than three neighbours.
  • A dead cell becomes a live cell if it has exactly three neighbours.
Neighbours are defined in all 8 directions not just cardinals.

The figure shown is the Gosper glider gun, creating 'gliders'.
